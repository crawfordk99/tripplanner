Overview

Purpose

To provide the perfect summer trip schedule! Our experienced traveling team will put take care of all of the planning so you can spend more time enjoying your trips!

Audience

We really want to focus on college/young adults. Those that don't have a lot of experience going on trips, but really want to make as many memories as possible. We want our customers to always look back fondly on just how much fun their trips were, and how we helped maximize that fun.

Content

Home page

Why Us? Although we welcome all to this site, we are especially targeting young adults/college students. We came up with this site with them in mind. It’s a unique time-period in their lives after they turn adults and have more freedom, and in a lot of cases, more money (at least compared to high school…), to go and make some special memories before family or a career, or both get in the way. Whether you want to make those memories with a special someone, a few friends, or a large group, we want to take away the planning part from you (for the most part) so you can spend more time just making memories and not stressing about the logistics. Especially with larger groups it can make planning and organizing the trips stressful, and take away from the fun somewhat, and we don’t want that to happen to you (we were once college students too...). We want you to be able to look back with nothing but a smile on your face on how fun this time of life was for you.

Images for the Home page

Your part

What we need from you Where you’re located, group size, the general area you want your trips around (if you have specific locations in that area you want to go to include them), time period you want us to plan for (like the whole semester? Just for the second half? Or just 1 particular weekend?), how often you want to go on trips if you plan on going on as many as possible, road trip or flight (if you have an airport preference, tell us), and a general budget for the trip (under 100, 100-500, 500-1000, etc.). We will include a section where you can make special requests that we will do our best to meet, but in general this is the general info we need from you to be able to plan out a good trip(s). If the area you want to go to is too expensive for the general budget you gave us, we will send an email to you to contact us to discuss other options if you feel like our plan would be too far over your budget. If you decide that you don’t want to try anywhere else, we will refund you the full amount. We are on your team as long as you’re willing to work with us.

Images for the Page 2

Our part

What we do for you Using Excel, we will create a spreadsheet plan for you. Included will be the date(s), location(s), travel time, possible travel costs (depends on whether a road trip, or flight, and if you have to rent a car/take public transportation), necessary items to bring (+costs for trips with special gear needed like camping), estimated costs for event (like if you need to pay to get in, or pay for certain benefits to get access to special events) estimated time at location/event (like if it’s a hike, how long will it take to get to the good spots on that hike), what to expect at location/event, nearby food spots to try (if not bringing packed food, will include estimated costs), any other special notes that we feel like you should know about, and estimated total costs and if they fall into your budget or not. We will also include a little check box column where you can mark on your own whether the trip was successful or not (a good thing to do so you can reference in the future when you are planning more trips). Included when we send you the spreadsheet will be suggested sites to visit if you decide you want to do your own research or change your mind about 1 of the locations and want to try something else.
