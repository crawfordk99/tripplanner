Our Part

What we do for you

Using Excel, we will create a spreadsheet plan for you. Included will be the date(s), location(s), travel time, possible travel costs (depends on whether a road trip, or flight, and if you have to rent a car/take public transportation), necessary items to bring (+costs for trips with special gear needed like camping), estimated costs for event (like if you need to pay to get in, or pay for certain benefits to get access to special events) estimated time at location/event (like if itâ€™s a hike, how long will it take to get to the good spots on that hike), what to expect at location/event, nearby food spots to try (if not bringing packed food, will include estimated costs), any other special notes that we feel like you should know about, and estimated total costs and if they fall into your budget or not. We will also include a little check box column where you can mark on your own whether the trip was successful or not (a good thing to do so you can reference in the future when you are planning more trips). Included when we send you the spreadsheet will be suggested sites to visit if you decide you want to do your own research or change your mind about 1 of the locations and want to try something else.
