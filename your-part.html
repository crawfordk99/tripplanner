Your Part

What we need from you

Where you’re located, group size, the general area you want your trips around (if you have specific locations in that area you want to go to include them), time period you want us to plan for (like the whole semester? Just for the second half? Or just 1 particular weekend?), how often you want to go on trips if you plan on going on as many as possible, road trip or flight (if you have an airport preference, tell us), and a general budget for the trip (under 100, 100-500, 500-1000, etc.). We will include a section where you can make special requests that we will do our best to meet, but in general this is the general info we need from you to be able to plan out a good trip(s). If the area you want to go to is too expensive for the general budget you gave us, we will send an email to you to contact us to discuss other options if you feel like our plan would be too far over your budget. If you decide that you don’t want to try anywhere else, we will refund you the full amount. We are on your team as long as you’re willing to work with us.
